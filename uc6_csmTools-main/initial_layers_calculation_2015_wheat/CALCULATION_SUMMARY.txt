Calculation Notes:
------------------
1. Water content (SH2O) varies by layer based on soil properties:
   Formula: SH2O = SLLL + (90/100) × (SDUL - SLLL)
   Where SLLL = wilting point, SDUL = field capacity

2. Nitrogen is distributed uniformly:
   - Total N: 100 kg/ha
   - Split: 90% NO3⁻ (3.10 ppm), 10% NH4⁺ (0.34 ppm)
   - Calculated from depth-weighted average bulk density: 1.45175 g/cm³

3. These values are ready for use in DSSAT .WHX file INITIAL_CONDITIONS section
