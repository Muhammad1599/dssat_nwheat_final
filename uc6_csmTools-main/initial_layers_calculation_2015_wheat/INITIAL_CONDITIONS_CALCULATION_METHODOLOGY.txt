3. WATER CALCULATION METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

3.1 Formula:
    SH2O (initial water content) = SDUL × 0.90

    Where:
    - SH2O = Volumetric water content (cm³/cm³)
    - SDUL = Field capacity from soil file (cm³/cm³)
    - 0.90 = 90% of field capacity

3.2 Calculation Steps:

    Layer 0-5 cm:
      SH2O = 0.236 × 0.90 = 0.2124 → 0.212 cm³/cm³

    Layer 5-15 cm:
      SH2O = 0.246 × 0.90 = 0.2214 → 0.221 cm³/cm³

    Layer 15-30 cm:
      SH2O = 0.252 × 0.90 = 0.2268 → 0.227 cm³/cm³

    Layer 30-60 cm:
      SH2O = 0.262 × 0.90 = 0.2358 → 0.236 cm³/cm³

    Layer 60-100 cm:
      SH2O = 0.276 × 0.90 = 0.2484 → 0.248 cm³/cm³

    Layer 100-200 cm:
      SH2O = 0.275 × 0.90 = 0.2475 → 0.248 cm³/cm³

3.3 Verification:
    Total water (mm) = Σ(SH2O × layer thickness × 10)
    
    Where:
    - layer thickness in cm
    - factor of 10 converts cm to mm (1 mm = 1 L/m²)
    
    Calculation:
      0-5 cm:   0.212 × 5 × 10 = 10.6 mm
      5-15 cm:  0.221 × 10 × 10 = 22.1 mm
      15-30 cm: 0.227 × 15 × 10 = 34.1 mm
      30-60 cm: 0.236 × 30 × 10 = 70.8 mm
      60-100 cm: 0.248 × 40 × 10 = 99.2 mm
      100-200 cm: 0.248 × 100 × 10 = 248.0 mm
      ------------------------------------------
      Total: 484.8 mm

4. NITROGEN CALCULATION METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

4.1 Target:
    Total inorganic N = 100 kg/ha
    Distributed across 6 layers with depth-varying concentrations

4.2 Starting Point:
    Previous values totaled 96.39 kg/ha
    Required increase factor = 100 / 96.39 = 1.0374 (3.74% increase)

4.3 Formula for N Concentration:
    N (kg/ha) = SNH4/SNO3 (μgN/g) × BD (g/cm³) × layer thickness (cm) × 0.1
    
    Where:
    - SNH4/SNO3 = Nitrogen concentration in μgN/g soil
    - BD = Bulk density in g/cm³
    - layer thickness = thickness of layer in cm
    - 0.1 = Conversion factor (μgN/g × g/cm³ × cm × 0.1 = kg/ha)
    
    Conversion factor derivation:
    - 1 μgN/g × 1 g/cm³ × 1 cm = 1 μgN/cm²
    - 1 μgN/cm² × (1 kg/10⁹ μg) × (10⁸ cm²/ha) = 0.1 kg/ha

4.4 Calculation Process:

    Step 1: Determine proportional increase
      Factor = 100 / 96.39 = 1.0374
    
    Step 2: Scale previous values
      Previous SNH4 values: [2.0, 1.6, 1.3, 1.0, 0.8, 0.7]
      Previous SNO3 values: [4.0, 3.5, 3.2, 3.0, 2.6, 2.3]
      
      New SNH4 = Previous × 1.0374
      New SNO3 = Previous × 1.0374
    
    Step 3: Round to practical values
      SNH4: [2.07, 1.66, 1.35, 1.04, 0.83, 0.73] → [2.1, 1.7, 1.3, 1.0, 0.8, 0.7]
      SNO3: [4.15, 3.63, 3.32, 3.11, 2.70, 2.39] → [4.1, 3.6, 3.3, 3.1, 2.7, 2.4]

4.5 Verification Calculation:

    Layer 0-5 cm:
      NH4-N = 2.1 × 1.29 × 5 × 0.1 = 1.35 kg/ha
      NO3-N = 4.1 × 1.29 × 5 × 0.1 = 2.64 kg/ha
    
    Layer 5-15 cm:
      NH4-N = 1.7 × 1.30 × 10 × 0.1 = 2.21 kg/ha
      NO3-N = 3.6 × 1.30 × 10 × 0.1 = 4.68 kg/ha
    
    Layer 15-30 cm:
      NH4-N = 1.3 × 1.32 × 15 × 0.1 = 2.57 kg/ha
      NO3-N = 3.3 × 1.32 × 15 × 0.1 = 6.53 kg/ha
    
    Layer 30-60 cm:
      NH4-N = 1.0 × 1.34 × 30 × 0.1 = 4.02 kg/ha
      NO3-N = 3.1 × 1.34 × 30 × 0.1 = 12.46 kg/ha
    
    Layer 60-100 cm:
      NH4-N = 0.8 × 1.39 × 40 × 0.1 = 4.45 kg/ha
      NO3-N = 2.7 × 1.39 × 40 × 0.1 = 15.01 kg/ha
    
    Layer 100-200 cm:
      NH4-N = 0.7 × 1.40 × 100 × 0.1 = 9.80 kg/ha
      NO3-N = 2.4 × 1.40 × 100 × 0.1 = 33.60 kg/ha
    
    Totals:
      Total NH4-N = 1.35 + 2.21 + 2.57 + 4.02 + 4.45 + 9.80 = 24.40 kg/ha
      Total NO3-N = 2.64 + 4.68 + 6.53 + 12.46 + 15.01 + 33.60 = 74.92 kg/ha
      Total Inorganic N = 24.40 + 74.92 = 99.32 kg/ha ≈ 100 kg/ha
